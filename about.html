About Me

I work at the intersection of remote sensing, GIS, and ecology, with a focus on understanding landscapes and wildlife through spatial data.

My interests include satellite remote sensing, UAV-based monitoring, ecological modeling, and building reproducible spatial workflows.

Outside of Work

Outside of research, I enjoy traveling, skiing, surfing, and spending time in remote environments. I’m especially interested in seasonal fieldwork and projects that blend science with exploration.
